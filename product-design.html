Product Design and Entrepreneurship Projects

Title: KoalaCare - Wearable Nocturnal Hypoglycemia Alert for Diabetics

Purpose: Final project for TEMG4950W - Healthcare Startup at HKUST

Instructor: Prof. Betty Lin

Duration: February 2017 - May 2017

Team Members: Shivang G, Shiv M, Arnold S, Lucas G

Result: First place winner. Overall course Grade A+

Project Brief
Design a product, create business plan and run Facebook advertisement campaign for a health care startup.

Project Overview
Based on interviews and surveys conducted with health care stake holders, we designed multiple products before shortlisting a dental health care solution and a wearable for diabetics. For our final project we chose the wearable as it showed greater promise in a real life setting.
