Title: Cybersecurity and Connected Cars - Current State and Future Threats

Purpose: Final project for COMP4632 - Practicing Cybersecurity at HKUST

Instructor: Prof. Ricci Ieong

Duration: Sep 2016 - Dec 2016

Team Members: Shivang G, Wai Kin W, Kwok Fai P

Result: Overall course Grade A+

Project Brief
To review a topic in the field of cybersecurity.

Project Overview
With a rise in the popularity of connected cars, the threat of compromised security in a car due to hacking has grown exponentially. We reviewed the current state of cybersecurity in connected cars and made some predictions about the future.
