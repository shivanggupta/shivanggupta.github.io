Final Year Project

Title: Bots as Personal Caregivers for Diabetics

Team Members: Shivang Gupta and Allen Fok

Supervisor: Prof. Sung Kim

Duration: September 2016 - April 2017

Purpose: Final year project for HKUST CSE Bachelor's

Result: A Grade, Semi-finalist HKUST President's Cup

Problem Statement
Diabetics have to monitor several factors about their health and existing apps are not able to provide an easy to use, ubiquitous solution.

Proposed Solution
To design and implement a Facebook Messenger chatbot that can provide a conversational interface to assist diabetics in disease management.
